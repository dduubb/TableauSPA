Starting Page

Use this tool to search for tax increases or decreases to any of Cook County’s 1.8 million property tax bills.

Enter a name, address or Property Index Number (PIN) in the search box to get the results for a specific tax bill.

The results show government taxing agencies by name; how much the property owner owed to each agency this year and last; and how much the amounts changed.

More Info

These two numbers are rarely identical. That’s because assessments — the estimated value of a property used to determine how much of the overall tax tab each property owner pays — change from one year to the next. For tax year 2022, billed in 2023, all suburban properties north of North Avenue were reassessed, leading to significant changes in how much of the tax burden each property owner owed.
